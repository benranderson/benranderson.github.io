Hi there. I'm Duarte (du-art).

I'm a technologist, born and raised in sunny Lisbon, now based in Copenhagen. My work lies in the intersection of Machine Learning & AI, Data, Software, and People. I'm in love with Technology, and how it can improve people's lives.

In the past, I've worked in Consumer Electronics, Public Institutions, Big Three Management Consulting, and YC-backed startups. The common thread? Solving hard problems end-to-end.

My work is greatly shaped by my background and education. I've studied at the Superior Technical Institute of Lisbon, the Beijing Institute of Technology, and the Technical University of Denmark.

When I'm not solving hard problems, you can find me geeking out about new gadgets, writing code, reading, taking photos, or running.

If you want to learn more about my work, you can browse my resume, my talks, or my consulting page.
